Overview


HGRIC is the Human Genetics Research Informatics Core, housed in the biomedical research tower at The Ohio State University. It serves hundreds of researchers by maintaining a consortium database with more than thirty thousand patients and millions of variants. It processes and adjudicates the prognosis of these variants searching for an asymptomatic pattern causing dilated cardiomyopathy (DCM). I have been currently working at this position for about five years and am currently building their next generation datamanagement platform.



Technology

The platform is build in django that servers as the backend and react which serves as the front end. Currently, I have built an authentication layer where all users will login with a keycloak authentication server, table / navigation react application as well as a form system that users can leverage to create, read, update or delete data. In the coming weeks, as of May 5th, 2024, I will be sure to update this page with gifs to illustrate the features I have been working on. The will all be in development as the finished project won't launch until September of 2024.
