Synthesizer Recorded Sessions

Micheal Vawlter: Modular Synth / Guitar

Thane: Piano / Drums

Charles Stevenson: Modular Synth

This jam was recorded at Thane/Michael’s studio, where I wrote a patch to get back into modular synth music after focusing on music theory and piano over the summer. Inspired by a trip to upper Michigan, I started the track with a drone, and built the melody and harmony in the style of Zelda’s Kakariko Village, in the key of B-flat.
This was our second jam session at Kerouac, where we aimed to build a community by inviting other musicians. Though it took a while to find our groove, the music flowed well, but an unplanned addition on piano wasn’t fully captured, highlighting the need for better preparation for future public jams.
Our first jam session together was planned the previous November, emerging from a growing community called Modsquad in Columbus, Ohio. We had a great turnout, with some fascinating visuals provided by Ian’s modular visual rig. Overall, the event showed real potential for hosting public electronic music jam sessions.

Charles Stevenson
